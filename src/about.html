About

​We are a small tech startup from California founded by Cybersecurity professionals with over 20 years of Industry experience in the IT GRC domain.

We provide quality IT GRC Consulting services to help our clients establish functional ISMS and Governance frameworks. These include gap analysis, risk assessment or security testing, followed by authoring necessary policy, process or technical documentation. We provide retainer services to ensure that our clients are able to take full advantage of the implemented frameworks and processes

Established in 2016

​We founded this tech startup in 2016 with the objective to develop and offer the ultimate IT GRC solution. We have been developing the solution since and currently we are in the 3rd generation offering based on the customer feedbacks after the consumption of the prior two generations
