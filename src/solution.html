Solution

GRC-Now is a comprehensive IT GRC Solution that enables you to efficiently govern the Cybersecurity aspects of your Organization's Infrastructure and IT Assets. The solution helps you to publish and maintain Information Security policy current, organize the portfolio of your Organization's IT Assets and Suppliers, perform Business Impact Assessments on them, identify Policy Exceptions and Risks within these resources and govern them through their existence

Policy Publication

GRC-Now is a content management solution that helps you to author, review and publish Information Security Policy and then follow them through the refresh cycle. It allows you to delegate control level ownership thus simplifying the complex governance process

Policy Exceptions

Best way to enrich Security Awareness is by empowering your users to identify and submit policy exceptions at the operational level. GRC-Now is great in gathering the exceptions across the Organization and help manage them balancing with overall risk

Risk Management

Organizations need to take risk to be successful and meet their goals but would need GRC-Now solution to have a holistic view of all technology risks across the Organization and their fluctuations to steer the Organizational goals toward success
